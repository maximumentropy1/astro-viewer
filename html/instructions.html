Instructions Page

To use the night sky simulation:

  1. Click and drag to move around the sky
  2. To view an object, click on the red symbol appearing next to its name. To close the object window, just click anywhere else on the simulation.
  3. The numbers at the top left of the simulation are the mouse's celestial coordinates. These can be used to locate various deep sky objects.
